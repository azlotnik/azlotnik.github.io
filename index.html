Anatoly Zlotnik

Los Alamos National Laboratory

Applied Math & Plasma Physics

I am a staff scientist at Los Alamos National Laboratory in the Applied Math and Plasma Physics group. My research interests are in dynamical systems, scientific computing, control theory, and optimization. My current projects involve modeling, design, and optimal control of large-scale energy transmission systems, in particular natural gas pipelines.

The Advanced Network Science Initiative is looking for graduate students and postdocs with research interests in optimization, control theory, applied math, network science, and energy systems. Does working with the brightest minds in the world on the nation’s critical energy challenges excite you? Contact me here.
